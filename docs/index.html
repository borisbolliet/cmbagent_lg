Interactive claim-map of a research paper

A knowledge-graph view of the claims, their interdependence, and the datasets behind them — extracted from Pelicano et al. 2026 (IL-1 CAFs / STAT1 / pancreatic cancer). Built with cmbagent_lg + denario.

3D · rotatable

Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom. Hover a node for the full claim. (Needs internet.)
2D · self-contained

2D view →

Pan & zoom, tick-box filters by node/edge type. Works offline.

Nodes: Claims (teal) & the Datasets (brown) that support them. Edges: DEPENDS_ON (claim → claim) and SUPPORTED_BY (claim → dataset). Use the legend checkboxes to toggle node/edge types. Datasets with a red border have no public accession — the paper's own sequencing data is not yet deposited.

Generated from the paper's OCR by an LLM claim-extraction pipeline, stored in a Postgres+AGE+pgvector knowledge graph, and rendered with vis-network / 3d-force-graph. Source: github.com/borisbolliet/cmbagent_lg.
